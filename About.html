About Archangel Project

A place where you can reconnect and converse with people whom you share the same experiences with. Archangel Project.

A Safe Haven

The Archangel Project is a Non-Profit and Non-Governmental Organization that has the sole purpose of being a safe haven for those individuals unfortunate enough to be subjected to the horrors of abuse, maltreatment, violence, and the likes. Through the collective efforts of the founding members, team officers, and countless volunteers, the project now has a nationwide influence and global gathering of millions of people from different places. All of them, experiencing the same pain.
